Why we are & Where we are going.

Established: 2015

Vision

The idea behind LightPoint Development was conceived in 2015. We are a team consisting of a graphics designer, web designer, web developer, programmer and an Internet Marketing specialist. We all have a passion for and a mission to use the skills that we have been given and we have developed to bring to non-profits and ministries needing assistance creating and / or maintaining a presence within the World Wide Web via sites and apps, social media and more.

Together we bring years of experience with an arsenal of abilities to give you the boost you need to further your own vision and mission around the world.

Working together we will help you develop and design your plan, your dreams and your ideas and bring them to fruition.

A combination of the new and old we each bring to the mission field the best of all that we have to offer. Continually we strive to stay current with skills, continuing education and seminars giving you the confidence that we can provide you with the best.

Mission

To bring the world to those companies and organizations in need of reaching out to them whether by hand-held device or computer. In this day, mobile users now outpace desktop accessing of the Internet. We want to help bring outdated sites up to current standards and activate those organizations who have yet reached out through this medium.
